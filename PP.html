Privacy Policy

At HGZ Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services or interact with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you:

  • Make inquiries or request information about our products or services.
  • Place an order or make a purchase.
  • Sign up for our newsletter or marketing communications.
  • Contact us for support or customer service.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders and requests.
  • To communicate with you and provide customer support.
  • To send you promotional materials, newsletters, and updates.
  • To improve our products, services, and website functionality.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers and business partners who assist us in delivering our products and services.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • With your consent or as otherwise disclosed at the time of data collection.

Security of Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices and services. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
